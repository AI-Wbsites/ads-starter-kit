# Client questionnaire — {{CLIENT_NAME}}

## 1. The business

1. What is the business/organisation name, and how should it appear on the site?
2. In one sentence, what does the business do?
3. What are the products or services you want the site to promote?
4. Who are your three biggest competitors? Links welcome.
5. What makes you different from them (your USP)?
6. How would you describe your brand's personality in 3–5 words? (e.g. bold, calm, playful, premium, trustworthy)

## 2. Audience

1. Who is the ideal visitor/customer? (age range, location, role, interests)
2. What problem are they trying to solve when they visit your site?
3. What do you want them to feel within the first 5 seconds?
4. What's the one action you most want them to take? (book a call, buy, sign up, call you, etc.)

## 3. Goals & success

1. What does this website need to achieve in the first 3 months? (leads, sales, awareness, bookings…)
2. How will you measure whether it's working?
3. Any existing website? If yes, what works and what doesn't about it?

## 4. Pages & content

1. What pages do you want? (e.g. Home, About, Services, Portfolio, Blog, Contact)
2. Do you have the content written, or do you need help with copy?
3. Do you have photos/videos? Or do we need stock/illustration?
4. Any legal pages required? (Privacy, Terms, Cookie, Impressum)

## 5. Visual direction

1. Share 3–5 websites you love the look of. What specifically do you like about each?
2. Share 1–2 websites you dislike. What puts you off?
3. Any colours that MUST be included? Any colours to AVOID?
4. Any existing logo, fonts, or brand guidelines? Please attach.
5. Do you want animations/motion? (subtle / moderate / bold)
6. Any imagery style preference? (photography / illustration / 3D / minimalist / maximalist)

## 6. Technical

1. Do you have a domain? If yes, what is it? If not, do you need help registering one?
2. Current hosting, if any?
3. Do you need a contact form? Where should submissions go? (email, CRM)
4. Do you need e-commerce? Bookings? Memberships?
5. Any third-party integrations? (Mailchimp, Calendly, HubSpot, Stripe, etc.)
6. Do you need multilingual support?
7. Do you need analytics? (GA4, Plausible, etc.)
8. Do you have existing social media accounts to link?

## 7. Process

1. Who on your side gives final approval?
2. What's your ideal launch date?
3. What's the budget range? (to scope scale, not to haggle)
4. Will you need ongoing maintenance after launch?

## 8. Anything else?

Anything we haven't asked that we should know?
